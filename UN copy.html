About the Project

The student debt crisis is an ongoing problem of the United States. Not only are students and graduates struggling to pay back their loans, but also feeling a lack of direction in their careers. In this research project, I explore the topic of institutional messaging and how colleges "overvalue" the college degree when compared to future outcomes of obtaining one. View my slide deck below.

Additional Information

Arbitrage is the simultaneous purchase and sale of an asset to profit from an imbalance in the price. Education arbitrage is the imbalance of money spent and earned between the price of education (in this case, higher education) and a successful work career. In this project, I define the implications of this imbalance.
