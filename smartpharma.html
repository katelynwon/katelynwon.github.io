Enhancing Drug Adherence and Efficacy with Digital Medicine

Using Digital Pills

Details

  • Date:February 7, 2021

  • Topic:Digital Medicine

  • Affiliation:The Knowledge Society

About the Project

My colleagues of The Knowledge Society (TKS) and I participated in a hackathon hosted by TKS. We were prompted to execute an idea/project combining two or more emerging technologies within 24 hours. Our team's two areas of focus were Nanotechnology and Personalized Medicine. We had decided that one of many hospitals' pressing problems was drug nonadherence, or a patient's decision to not adhere to their medication plan. We thought digital pills synced with a mobile application could solve this problem that doctors face everyday.

Additional Information

Our solution will provide doctors and healthcare providers the ability to access drug adherence data through a mobile application integrated with the patient's electronic health record. In addition, the mobile application will notify the user when their perscribed medicine is released within the body by the digital pill.
